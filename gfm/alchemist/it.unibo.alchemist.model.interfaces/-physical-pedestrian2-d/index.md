A [PhysicalPedestrian](../-physical-pedestrian/index.md) in an euclidean bidimensional space. This pedestrian has a circular [comfortArea](comfort-area.md) of radius equal to its shape radius plus a [comfortRay](comfort-ray.md). This is derived from [the work of Pelechano et al](https://bit.ly/3e3C7Tb).
